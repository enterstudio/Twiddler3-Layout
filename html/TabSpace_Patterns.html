   OOOR    L Toggle
   OOOM    R Toggle
   OOOL S L Clickx2
About the logic of this layout:
  • O, Right, Middle and Left are prioritized in that order.
  • Pressed-buttons are listed in a TOP-DOWN order (LEFT-RIGHT in this 4x(ORML) notation).
    • This logic creates groups of look-alike combinations so that similitude and differences can be spotted.
